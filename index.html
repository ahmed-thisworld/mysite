About Us

Welcome to Salwa couture, where tradition meets modem elegance through the timeless art of Lucknow. Our jourmey began with a passion for preserving and promoting the intricate craft of threadwork, a centuries-old embroidery technique from Lucknow, the heart of Uttar Pradesh. Each piece we create is a tribute to the rich cultural heritage of India, blending the sophistication of the Mughal era with contemporary fashion.

At Salwa Couture, we are proud to offer beautifully handcrafted Lucknow that carry forward the legacy of Chikankari embroidery. This delicate, intricate work, once adored by Mughal royalty and the Nawabs of Awadh, is still created using traditional methods passed down through generations. Our skilled artisans meticulously hand-embroider each garment, ensuring that every piece tells a story of artistry, heritage and elegance. We source the finest fabrics, from lightweight cotton to luxurious silk, to provide you with comfort and style for every occasion. Whether you're seeking something casual for daily wear or an elegant piece for a special event our collections offer a range of designs that celebrate the timeless appeal of the thread work.
